Space Run

You are trying to find the essential battery(or coca colar) as quick as possible to surrive in the space station. But be aware of those guarding rotbot dogs.

There are 10 plaforms connected to space station. Every few seconds a new battery will appear on one of platforms and last for several seconds. You have rush to battery before running out of your energy. Meanwhile a robot dog will chase you and cause damage to you when bites you.

Gameplay

Player's energy keep losing after game start. He has to use keyboard to control player move to the energy battery. If he has no more energy before finding next battery or killed by robot dog, game ends.

Assets

It can be done with the same resources from space-dog.blend for the game.
